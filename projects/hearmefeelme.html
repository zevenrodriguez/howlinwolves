Hear me / Feel me

Artist

Zevensuy Rodriguez

Location

projekt722 Gallery, Williamsburg/Brooklyn, New York

Materials

wood, wood veneer

About

Hear me / Feel Me are set pieces that are meant to play on words and senses. The pieces convey an auditory (Morse Code) and tactile (Braille) into a light/visual language. The pieces are CNCed wooden boxes with covered in veneer. While the light is off it looks like a simple wooden box. The message is only displayed when the lights come on.
